The Scaling Trap

Most hospitality owners reach a plateau where they are working harder but seeing less free cash. In the early days, you managed by walking the floor. Now, with multiple units or seasonal swings, you are forced to manage by looking at reports.

The Visibility Gap:

Most reporting is historical and passive. It tells you where the money went, but it never tells you where it is going. This creates a dangerous environment where expansion decisions are made on gut feeling rather than capital readiness.
